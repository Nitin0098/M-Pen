# M-Pen (Patent Pending)
M-Pen is the most innovative and exciting project that I am currently working on.
It's the next generation human computer interaction device that bridges the gap between real world and digital world ingeniously!

M-Pen is basically a device that can capture handwriting in real time with great precision and accuracy (at least in theory)
and I'm currently working to make this theoractical device a reality.


Devices that can capture handwriting already exist so why we need M-Pen you may ask?

We need M-Pen because there is a huge gap in current technology, 
existing products usually require a external pad(XPPen, Wacom pen tablets) or special paper(sync pen, Livescribe) with patterns to work, the problem those external pads/tablets are limited by usable area and companies sell those books with special paper exclusively as very high prices. these problems prohibit general users and students from using this tech, who needs them most for digital notes taking, art and animations.
some devices exist that are not dependent of external pads and special surfaces like nuwa pen but since they use multiple high speed cameras to track position they cost atleast 250$ which is expensive and unaffordable to many students and general public.

M-pen is superior to existing products and devices because of its propriority tech and clever sensor mechanism, M-pen is not limited so special surfaces and tablets it can work on any smooth surface from paper to displays, and cost a fracton of what other products cost.

M-Pen has 2 varients,
1. M-Pen Ink : As the name suggests, it has ink and suppoosed to be used on any paper, m_pen can capture handwriting as you write and can send the data to smartphones for storage. Other use cases include Drawing, Art, Digital Signatures, Personalised Messages, etc.

2. M-Pen Inkless : similar to a stylus, it can work on displays (although it can work on any surface from paper to displays) it can be used to for digital art, animations or just like a stylus, the interesting point is since M-pen is the sensor, it can work even on non-touch displays like computer monitors or laptop displays, it has the potential to convert any non touch display to interactive touch display.
